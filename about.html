Right Space is Coimbatore's design-and-manufacture studio — pairing architectural interior design with our own furniture workshop for a level of control few can match.

Our Story

From a small workshop to South India's trusted studio

Right Space began with a simple conviction: interiors are only as good as the craft behind them. What started as a modest furniture workshop in Coimbatore has grown into a full-service interior design studio delivering turnkey homes, offices and commercial spaces across South India.

Because we design and manufacture under one roof, we control every detail — from the first moodboard to the final coat of finish. That's how we protect quality, timelines and your budget.

Founder's Message

"Good design should be lived in, not just looked at."

We don't chase trends. We design spaces around the people who use them — how they cook, host, work and rest. Our promise is honest craftsmanship, transparent pricing and a finish we're proud to put our name on.

— Founder, Right Space Interiors & Furniture
