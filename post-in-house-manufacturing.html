Why In-House Manufacturing Changes Everything

◷ 4 min read✎ Right Space Design Team◆ April 2026
On this page
The industry norm: outsourcing Quality control Timelines you can trust Honest pricing True customisation FAQs

Ask most interior design studios where your furniture is actually made, and the honest answer is: somewhere else, by someone else. Design is done in one place, manufacturing is sub-contracted to a vendor, and the studio hopes it comes back right. At Right Space, we do it differently — we design and build under one roof in our own Coimbatore factory. Here is why that matters to you.

The industry norm: outsourcing

Outsourcing creates a gap between the person who designed your piece and the person who builds it. Every hand-off is a chance for detail to be lost, quality to slip, and accountability to blur. When something goes wrong, the studio blames the vendor and the vendor blames the brief — and you are stuck in the middle.

When you own the factory, there's no one to pass the blame to. The quality is simply yours.

Quality control

Because our carpenters and finishers are our own team, we inspect quality at every stage — cut, assembly, finish and dispatch — against a single standard. There is no mystery about materials or shortcuts hidden inside a cabinet. What we specify is what gets built.

Timelines you can trust

Owning production means we control the queue. We are not waiting in line behind a vendor's other clients, so we can commit to a schedule and hold it. For a full-home interior or an office fit-out, that reliability is the difference between moving in on time and living with delays.

Honest pricing

No vendor mark-up sitting between you and the workshop means pricing is transparent. We give line-item quotes so you can see exactly what you are paying for and approve every change. It is one of the simplest ways we build trust.

True customisation

Catalogue furniture forces your home to fit the product. Owning the factory lets the product fit your home — exact dimensions, bespoke finishes, and details like the disappearing storage in this villa or the joinery behind a warm-minimal interior. If you can draw it, we can usually build it.

Key takeaways

  • Most studios outsource; the hand-offs cost you quality and accountability.
  • In-house means one standard, checked at every stage.
  • We control the production queue, so timelines hold.
  • No vendor mark-up means transparent, line-item pricing.
  • Everything is truly custom to your space.
