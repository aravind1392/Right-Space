Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Right Space Interiors & Furniture ("Right Space", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you submit a consultation request, newsletter sign-up, callback or WhatsApp enquiry, we collect the details you provide — such as your name, phone number, email address, project type, budget range and any message. We may also collect basic, non-identifying analytics data (pages visited, device type) to improve the site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a consultation or quote.
  • To communicate about your project and our services.
  • To send occasional updates or design ideas, if you opt in to our newsletter.
  • To improve our website and services.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with team members and trusted service providers who help us deliver your project, and where required by law.

Data retention & your rights

We keep enquiry data only as long as needed for the purposes above. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
